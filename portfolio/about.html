ABOUT

Designer & Analyst based in Colorado

Hi, I'm Laila

I’m a third-year student at CU Boulder pursuing a B.S.B.A. in Business Analytics and Information Management with a minor in Creative Technology and Design. I design at the intersection of data, creativity, and human experience.

My background in analytics gives me a strong foundation in data management, visualization, and strategic decision-making, and I use these skills to inform thoughtful, human-centered design. I believe that great design is about clarity, empathy, and understanding the “why” behind people’s behaviors. I focus on creating experiences that feel intuitive, purposeful, and human. I’m especially passionate about using design as a form of storytelling and transforming complex information into experiences that are accessible, engaging, and meaningful.
