Alpha Kappa Psi · Gamma Zeta

Design Chair · Marketing Committee · Spring 2025

Learn more

As Design Chair for the Gamma Zeta chapter, I developed an “alpine retro” visual brand theme and produced all design assets for recruitment and the semester, including video content, social media graphics, print collateral, and merchandise. In this role, I collaborated on the marketing committee to shape the chapter’s creative direction, while executing across branding, digital, and physical touchpoints. I primarily designed in Canva, and also used Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro to support more advanced production needs.

myworld App

Wireframes + event flows from the concept stage.

Learn more

myworld is an app concept and design I created in response to the prompt of “solving climate change.” The app allows users to log daily activity across categories such as recycling, driving, taking public transit, online shopping, and more. By tracking these behaviors, myworld identifies which areas of a user’s lifestyle have the greatest environmental impact and which have the least. The goal is to provide accessible insights that encourage gradual, meaningful lifestyle changes—helping users make more informed choices and contribute to a healthier planet over time.
