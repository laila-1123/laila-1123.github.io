Favorites

I added a “Favorite” feature for generated images. There is a heart button next to the generate new images button.clicking it toggles the image into a Favorites gallery that appears right under the “Generated Images” section. Users can hover over images they've already favorited to remove them. They can favorite as many images as they want.
