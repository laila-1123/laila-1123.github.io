Resources for Readers in Boulder

At Book Club Hub, we want to make reading accessible and affordable for everyone. Here are some of the best places in Boulder to find books, whether you’re looking to borrow, buy, or discover something new.

Libraries

Boulder Public Library (BPL)

The city’s main hub for free reading materials. With a wide selection of books, e-books, and audiobooks, plus plenty of quiet reading spaces, BPL is perfect for readers on any budget. They also host author talks, workshops, and community events.

CU Boulder Libraries

For students, faculty, and community members with access, the CU Boulder library system offers millions of books, research materials, and study-friendly spaces. It’s also a great spot to find more academic or rare titles.

Bookstores

The Bookworm

A local favorite for used books, The Bookworm is the perfect place to pick up affordable reads while supporting a small business. You’ll find everything from modern bestsellers to quirky hidden gems.

Trident Booksellers & Café

An independent bookstore and cozy café rolled into one. Trident offers a curated mix of new and used titles, and it’s a great place to grab a coffee while browsing.

Boulder Book Store

Located on Pearl Street, this iconic three-story indie bookstore has been a community staple for decades. With an extensive collection across all genres, it’s a must-visit for any Boulder reader.

E-Reading

Libby (via Boulder Public Library & CU Libraries)

With your library card, you can download the free Libby app to access thousands of e-books and audiobooks at no cost. Perfect for commuters, travelers, or anyone who prefers digital reading.

Amazon Prime Reading

If you’re an Amazon Prime member, you automatically get access to Prime Reading, which offers one free e-book each month along with a rotating library of popular titles.

Project Gutenberg

A massive online collection of free public domain books. Great for classics like Pride and Prejudice or Moby-Dick.

Hoopla Digital

Another service available with many library memberships, offering e-books, audiobooks, comics, and even movies.
